About Hebrew Alefbet Dojo

Hebrew Alefbet Dojo is a free web app that helps you learn the Hebrew alphabet (alefbet) and vowel marks (niqqud) through quiz-based repetition. It's built for short, focused practice sessions that make memorizing the script easier.

For many learners, the first obstacle to reading the Hebrew Bible in the original language is the script itself. Hebrew is written from right to left, is primarily consonantal, and uses a system of vowel marks that takes time to get used to. Before diving into grammar, learners benefit from repeated exposure to the letters and signs.

Alefbet Dojo is designed as a focused practice tool for this first stage of Biblical Hebrew. The quiz format keeps the pace fast and engaging, making it easy to practice regularly—even in short bursts—so beginners can take their first steps toward reading.

No registration is required, and it's completely free. Whether you're a seminary student, learning in a church, synagogue, or classroom setting, studying on your own, or simply interested in ancient scripts, you're welcome to give it a try.
