Privacy Policy

Effective as of March 1, 2026

Our Commitment

Shingo Kumagai is committed to providing a safe, distraction-free learning environment for all users of Hebrew Alefbet Dojo. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled through the Service.

For Educational Settings and Minors

The Service is designed to be safe for use in seminaries, universities, churches, Sunday schools, and by younger learners. No registration is required, and normal use of the Service does not require users to provide personal information such as names or email addresses.

Information We Handle

1. Browser Storage (localStorage)

Your selected font, quiz mode, and language preference may be saved in your browser's localStorage. This data remains on your device and is not sent to the Service.

2. Analytics (Google Analytics)

The Service uses Google Analytics to better understand how the Service is used and to improve the user experience. Google Analytics may collect anonymized or aggregated usage data through cookies and similar technologies. The Service does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name or email address through Google Analytics. You can opt out by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. Please see Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

3. Contact Form (Google Forms)

If you submit a message through the contact form, the information you provide (such as your name and message) may be collected and stored through Google Forms and used only to respond to your inquiry. This information is not sold or shared with third parties except as required by law.

Sharing of Information

Shingo Kumagai does not sell, trade, or otherwise disclose user information to third parties, except as required by law or as described in this Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will take effect when they are posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about privacy, please use the contact form.
